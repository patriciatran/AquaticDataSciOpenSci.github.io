Advocating Open Data and Open Science in the Aquatic Sciences

The grass-root networkworks DSOS and AEMON-J (Aquatic Ecosystem MOdeling Network - Junior) are joining forces this year to organize a 2nd 'Virtual Summit: Incorporating Data Science and Open Science in Aquatic Research' that includes fascinating presentations about aquatic research including open data and open science approaches, and multiple days of exciting workshops that include state-of-the-art keynote talks with live hands-on coding exercises.
